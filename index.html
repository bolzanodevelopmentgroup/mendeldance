About Us

Annette
Anri

We teach the art of dance — Ballet, Modern, Hip Hop, Spanish and more — with professionalism, with love and with care to all ages, from toddlers to adults.

With professionalism

We have the training, experience and skills to do our job well and are Registered Teachers of:

  • The Royal Academy of Dance, one of the world’s most influential dance education organisations.
  • The Spanish Dance Society, the world’s largest society for the advancement of Spanish dance in all its forms.
  • ADC, the alternative Modern- and Hip Hop dance education organisation.

We are committed to Continued Professional Development, consistently updating and improving our knowledge and skills.

With love

We can be the best but without love we are nothing and it would profit us nothing.If, however, we are fulfilling the royal law found in Scripture, “Love your neighbour as yourself,” we are doing well. Therefore we treat our students the same way we want them to treat us.

With care

We make every effort to teach dance correctly, safely, without causing emotional and physical damage to our students. We believe every student must have an equal opportunity to use their God given talents to such an extent that it enables them to reach their full potential and to become the best dancer that they can be.

ABOUT ANNETTE

  • Registered Member & Teacher with the Royal Academy of Dance (RAD).
  • Registered Member & lnstructor De Baile Espannol with the International Spanish Dance Society [ISDS).
  • Co-founder, Teacher & Examiner with the ADC Dance Company (ADC).
  • Co-founder & Principal Dancer with the C’mist Performing Arts Group

I was born on a farm just outside of Morgenzon, in Mpumalanga. My parents never doubted what career I would follow. They always said that before I could even walk I was the happiest when music was playing and I was moving my body on the rhythm thereof. But when I started riding wild horses my mother said it is time to start with Ballet classes.

My teaching career started at the age of 16 teaching Ballet at the Art School in Ermelo. A few years later I taught Spanish for Jose and Elaine Montoya, under PACT in Pretoria.

Looking back I am extremely grateful for many wonderful privileges and highlights.

  • Firstly for my daughter Anri, not only following in my footsteps but being my partner.
  • For every student impacted and trained.
  • Every parent I met over the years.
  • Dancing for the Jose Montoya Spanish Dance Company.
  • Performing on many national and international platforms in South Africa, Brazil, India, Israel, Jordan, Mauritius and Turkey.

If I could turn the clock back I would have made the same choices.

ABOUT ANRI

  • Registered Member & Teacher with the Royal Academy of Dance (RAD).
  • Registered Member, Teacher & Examiner with the ADC Dance Company (ADC).
  • Registered Member & lnstructor De Baile Espannol with the International Spanish Dance Society ISDS).
  • Principal Dancer with the C’mist Performing Arts Group

My love for dancing started when I was “’keeping rhythm ’’ inside my mom’s belly. She taught till the final countdown that every dancer knows 5, 6, 7, 8….., and then it was almost too late. LOL.

I grew up in my mom’s dance studio and wanted to follow in her footsteps. Looking back I am extremely grateful for many wonderful privileges and highlights.

  • Firstly having my mom, Annette Erasmus, as my first ballet teacher.
  • For every student impacted and trained.
  • Instructed and trained by many acclaimed teachers, choreographers and examiners.
  • Performing at Super Skouspel with famous artists and movie stars.
  • Perform and choreograph on many national and international platforms in South Africa, Argentina, Armenia, Brazil, India, Israel and Mauritius.

Today being in my “happy place” I know I made the right choice to follow in my mom’s footsteps.
